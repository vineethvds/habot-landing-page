    10. Your Data Protection Rights Under Federal Decree-Law no. 45 of 2021

    You have the right to know that the UAE government provides data regulation that ultimately comprises a patchwork of various data provisions derived from its Penal code, Constitution, Cyber Crime Law.

    10.1. Right to obtain information
    You have the right to be told about what aspects of and how your Personal Data is being processed. Whilst there is no express requirement for businesses to use privacy notices, their use could assist in securing compliance with this requirement amongst others.

    10.2. Right to access
    You have the right to obtain access to your Personal Data.

    10.3. Right to request Personal Data transfer
    You have the right to request that the Personal Data is moved from one business to another (e.g. if they decide to change service provider).

    10.4. Right to correction or erasure
    You have the right to require inaccurate information about yourself to be corrected and have the right to have your Personal Data erased if it is no longer required for the purpose for which it was collected.

    10.5. Right to restrict or stop processing
    You may require businesses to restrict or stop processing their Personal Data in certain circumstances.

    10.6. Right to file a complaint
    You have the right to file a complaint with the UAE Data Office.

    10.7. Right to withdraw consent
    At any time you can withdraw consent they have given a business to process your Personal Data.
