    Habot Subscription Cancellation and Refund Policy

    Last update 12/12/2022

    1. The refund policy is outlined below:

    1.1 Payment for your subscription is charged and collected in advance. All subscriptions, monthly and annual are renewed after payment of the requisite Subscription fee on expiry of your earlier subscription period. You may cancel your subscription at any time by logging on to your account and following the cancellation steps.

    1.2 Canceling Your Subscriptions: Please note that when you cancel your subscription you will not receive any refunds for the leftover period of your plan. The cancellation will take effect at the end of the subscription plan period. You will continue to have access to your account until your paid plan period runs out.

    1.3 Subscription plans cannot be changed from Annual subscription plan to Monthly subscription plan and no refunds or credits for Fees or payments will be provided to you if you wish to change or cancel your Subscription Plan

    1.4 You will receive an email and SMS from us confirming cancellation of your subscription to the services.

    1.5 Payment once made can only be refunded in the following circumstances:

    a. Technical Issues:
    If there are technical issues in the HABOT Application that have blocked your access to your account then Habot can consider a refund of the subscription. Habot’s decision in this regard will be final.

    b. Product or Service Issues:
    Your subscription will be refunded if the services have been canceled as a whole or in your specific case.

    i. Refunds may take time as required by the due processes concerning third party service providers.

    ii. Subject to the aforesaid, any refund shall also be subject to production of proof that the product or service is not delivered. You will have to make a request for any such refund with the required proofs, to your dedicated Live Chat Assistant (LCA)

    2. The refund will be processed manually, and after verification, if the claim is found valid and if the proofs are identified to be valid by us. The amount due will be refunded within a period of 21 Business Days on receipt of such claim.

    3. It may take 3-21 business days for the refund to show in your bank account, depending on your bank’s policy. We will not be responsible or liable for any delay, which may happen due to requirements with the banking channel.
