    Habot stands for Happy Robot.

    Habot is a web-based app designed to provide assistance to anyone who lives, works, or does business in the United Arab Emirates [UAE].

    SIGN UP
    How Do You Stand To Benefit?

    You can choose a specific plan based on whether you live, work or do business in the UAE. The document checklists are designed and updated accordingly.

    It keeps in one place for you, all the documents you need based on your above choice.

    A continuously updated checklist guides you to upload the required documents. You can also add other documents you think are relevant to your situation but are not on the checklist.

    You will start receiving reminders 90 days in advance of the expiry of each document.

    You can have them renewed for you via your Live Chat Assistant [LCA] or you can renew them yourself. You won’t miss out on timely renewals.

    As soon as you become a subscriber you get assigned a team of dedicated LCAs.

    Who Is Bringing Habot To You?

    Founded March 16, 2010. Served more than 10,000 inquiries since then.

    Also, you will be working with a 100% remote company that is headquartered in Dubai, UAE, with its 80+ full-time team members spread over India, the Philippines, Kenya, and Bangladesh. Giving you Live Chat availability for longer periods.
